Why IRC?

  • Enables quick discussion and collaboration without breaking workflow.
  • Logs of discussion can be valuable for future references
  • Open, Widely Supported Protocol
  • Easily extensible with other tools
  • Easy way to communicate with the OSS communities spread around the world

Existing IRC Clients

  • X-Chat, Linkinus, Irrsi, mIRC, IRCCloud

Problems we faced starting with IRC

  • Low internet bandwidth resulting in frequent disconnection.
  • No internet connection to stay online on IRC
  • Blocked IRC ports (office/college)
  • No persistent logs on existing clients

Waartaa

  • Beautiful web and native client
  • Accessible from anywhere and multiple devices
  • Centralized logging
  • 24x7 idling
  • Mail notifications when away
  • Search interface for chat logs
  • Realtime
